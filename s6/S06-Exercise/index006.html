Snapdrop

Ready to Share your local files with your friends?

Snapdrop is a local file sharing solution which completely works in your browser. A bit like Apple's Airdrop, but not only for Apple devices.

As featured in:

Amazing Features

Snapdrop is packed with features and possibilities, making it easy to share everything you have on your phone with your friends and family.

Open Source

App is developed using some of the best security protocols. It's open-source, so developers are continuously improving it.

Cross Platform

Snapdrop works in your browser. A bit like Apple's Airdrop, but not only for Apple devices. Windows, Linux, Android, IPhone, Mac - no problem at all!

Easy to Use

As long as both devices are on the same Wi-Fi, you can access Snapdrop and send your files.
